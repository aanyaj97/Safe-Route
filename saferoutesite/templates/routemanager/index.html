Welcome to our CMSC 122 Project: Safe-Route.

This project is designed to find the "safest" walking route for a given start and end destination. Our algorithm takes in to account weather conditions and time of travel and computes the likelihood of a crime occuring on a given path given crime history in the area.

Our program only requires entry of a start and end address (if travelling now), or, if you would like to see a route for a different day, you may enter in that date, time, or weather conditions.

Please enter in your desired start and finish address within the City of Chicago to see the safest route you can take between them.
